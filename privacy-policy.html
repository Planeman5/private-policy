Privacy Policy for VR Backgrounds

1. Introduction

VR Backgrounds (the “App”) is a virtual reality application that places users in a seamless, looped video environment. We prioritize user privacy and aim to minimize any data collection. This policy outlines how we handle any information associated with your use of the App.

2. Data Collection and Use

VR Backgrounds does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The App does not require registration, nor does it collect data on usage, location, or other personal information. Any data necessary for the functioning of the App remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted externally.

3. Third-Party Services

Our App is distributed through the Meta Quest platform. Meta may collect certain information in accordance with their privacy policies. Please refer to Meta's Privacy Policy for details on data they may collect as part of your use of the Meta Quest platform.

4. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by VR Backgrounds, there are no data security risks associated with personal information.
